# Proctoring Guidelines

1. **Transparency**: The user must be aware they are being monitored. Show a "Trust Score" badge prominently.
2. **Non-blocking**: Do not stop the test due to proctoring flags. Record the anomalies.
3. **Multi-modal**: Proctoring consists of coding environment tracking (copy/paste), browser tracking (tab switch), and webcam tracking (face-api.js).
4. **Data Privacy**: Store video frames as metadata only, not the actual images or video streams.
